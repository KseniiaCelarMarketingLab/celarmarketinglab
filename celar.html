About the studio

Celar Marketing Lab exists because most businesses don’t need another marketer — they need a universe worth stepping into.

We’re a strategy-first studio based in the Netherlands, working across NL, BE, DE and FR markets, multilingually in English, Dutch, French and Russian. Every engagement starts with behavioral science and strategic thinking, not a moodboard.

We work with e-commerce brands, retail businesses and SMBs who are ready to be built properly — from positioning to the smallest pixel — instead of stitched together from templates and trends.
