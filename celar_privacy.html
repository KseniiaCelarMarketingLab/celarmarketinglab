Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Who we are

Celar Marketing Lab (“CML”, “we”, “us”) is a marketing studio based in the Netherlands, operating within the European Union. This policy explains how we collect, use and protect personal data when you visit celarmarketinglab.com or interact with our services.

What data we collect

  • Contact form submissions: name, email, website/Instagram handle, and message content
  • Quiz responses: your answers and, if provided, your email address
  • Purchase data processed via Stripe when you buy a digital product
  • Cookie preferences, stored locally in your browser

Legal basis for processing

Under GDPR, we process your data based on one or more of the following: your consent (e.g. cookie preferences, quiz email), the performance of a contract (e.g. delivering a purchased product), or our legitimate interest in responding to enquiries and improving our services.

Third parties we work with

  • Stripe — payment processing for digital products
  • Web3Forms — contact form and lead capture delivery
  • Vercel — website hosting and deployment
  • Calendly — call scheduling
  • Google Fonts — typography delivery

Your rights

Under GDPR you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request erasure of your data
  • Restrict or object to certain processing
  • Request data portability
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority
  • Not be subject to automated decision-making

Contact

For any privacy questions or requests, contact us at kseniia@celarlab.com.

Supervisory authority

If you believe your data protection rights have been violated, you may lodge a complaint with the Dutch supervisory authority, the Autoriteit Persoonsgegevens.
